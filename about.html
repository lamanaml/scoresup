Meet the Owner

Tiffany Melvin

Credit Negotiator

Fed up with the frustration of having bad credit, and seeing numerous unresolved inaccuracies on my credit profile, I wanted a change! Along with seeing problems with my financial portfolio, I noticed that many of my peers were experiencing the same setbacks. It was then that I decided to start the change I wanted to see!

I started the “Learn-Teach Method” to assist others with achieving their personal credit goals and maintaining healthy credit profiles through credit education and restoration. I have a long history with the credit industry. I started as a credit department manager where my sole purpose was to advocate for consumers and negotiate with various lenders to turn consumer denials into approvals! That experience developed my skills and enhanced my natural passion for advocacy.

After years of studying and perfecting my method, Scores Up Financial Services was birthed to meet the need I saw and do what I do best…NEGOTIATE!! Welcome to Scores Up Financial Services! I look forward to helping you on your journey to success!
